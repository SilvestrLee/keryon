## Context

Keryon is a Church Communications Platform.

Congregation is not intended to function as a traditional church membership management module.

Its purpose is to help church communicators know people, care for people, and communicate consistently.

## Decision

Keryon follows a Product-first Filament approach.

Congregation will behave as a communications-oriented people directory.

## Rationale

This approach aligns Keryon with modern SaaS products such as Notion, Linear and Buffer rather than traditional ERP or church management systems.

Congregation serves communications workflows rather than administrative workflows.
